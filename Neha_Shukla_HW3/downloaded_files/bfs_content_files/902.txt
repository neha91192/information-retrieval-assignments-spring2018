      <p>
       <b>
        Re-recording
       </b>
       is the process by which the
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Audio_track" title="Audio track">
        audio track
       </a>
       of a
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Film_soundtrack" title="Film soundtrack">
        film
       </a>
       or
       <a href="/wiki/Video_production" title="Video production">
        video production
       </a>
       is created. As sound elements are
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Audio_mixing_(film_and_television)" title="Audio mixing (film and television)">
        mixed
       </a>
       and combined together the process necessitates "re-recording" all of the audio elements, such as
       <a href="/wiki/Dialogue" title="Dialogue">
        dialogue
       </a>
       , music,
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sound_effects" title="Sound effects">
        sound effects
       </a>
       , by the sound re-recording mixer(s) to achieve the desired end result, which is the final soundtrack that the audience hears when the finished film is played.
      </p>

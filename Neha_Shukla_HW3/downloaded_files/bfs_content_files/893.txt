   <h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">
    Daily call sheet
   </h1>
   <div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
    <div class="noprint" id="siteSub">

    </div>

    <div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en">
     <div class="mw-parser-output">
      <p>
       The
       <b>
        daily call sheet
       </b>
       is a
       <a href="/wiki/Filmmaking" title="Filmmaking">
        filmmaking
       </a>
       term for the schedule crafted by the
       <a href="/wiki/Assistant_director" title="Assistant director">
        assistant director
       </a>
       , using the
       <a href="/wiki/Film_director" title="Film director">
        director
       </a>
       's shot list. It is issued to the cast and crew of a film production to inform them of where and when they should report for a particular day of
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Filming" title="Filming">
        filming
       </a>
       .
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
        <a href="#cite_note-1">
         [1]
        </a>
       </sup>
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">
        <a href="#cite_note-2">
         [2]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>
      <p>
       The production schedule is listed by
       <b>
        call time
       </b>
       , the time when people are expected to start work on a
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Film_set" title="Film set">
        film set
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <h2>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Information_found_on_call_sheets">
        Information found on call sheets
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
        </span>
        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Daily_call_sheet&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Information found on call sheets">
         edit
        </a>
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h2>
      <p>
       Call sheets include other useful information such as contact information (e.g. phone numbers of crew members and other contacts), the schedule for the day, which
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Scene_(film)" title="Scene (film)">
        scenes
       </a>
       and
       <a href="/wiki/Screenplay" title="Screenplay">
        script
       </a>
       pages are being shot, and the address of the shoot location. Call sheets have information about
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cast_member" title="Cast member">
        cast
       </a>
       transportation arrangements, parking instructions and safety notes.
      </p>
      <p>
       Call sheets may also provide logistical information regarding the
       <a href="/wiki/Filming_location" title="Filming location">
        location
       </a>
       . It is common to find such items as weather information, sunrise/sunset times, local hospitals, restaurants, and hardware stores on call sheets.
      </p>

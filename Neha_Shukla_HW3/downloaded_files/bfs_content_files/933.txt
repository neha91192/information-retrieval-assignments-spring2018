      <p>
       An
       <b>
        amateur
       </b>
       (
       <a href="/wiki/French_language" title="French language">
        French
       </a>
       <i>
        amateur
       </i>
       "lover of", from Old French and ultimately from Latin
       <i>
        amatorem
       </i>
       nom.
       <i>
        amator
       </i>
       , "lover") is generally considered a person attached to a particular pursuit, study, or science in a non-
       <a href="/wiki/Professional" title="Professional">
        professional
       </a>
       or unpaid manner.
      </p>

       <i>
        <b>
         Allá en el Norte
        </b>
       </i>
       (
       <a href="/wiki/English_language" title="English language">
        English language
       </a>
       :
       <i>
        <b>
         Somewhere in the North
        </b>
       </i>
       ) is a
       <a href="/wiki/1973_in_film" title="1973 in film">
        1973
       </a>
       <a href="/wiki/Argentina" title="Argentina">
        Argentine
       </a>
       <a href="/wiki/Musical_film" title="Musical film">
        musical
       </a>
       <a href="/wiki/Comedy_film" title="Comedy film">
        comedy film
       </a>
       directed by
       <a href="/wiki/Julio_Saraceni" title="Julio Saraceni">
        Julio Saraceni
       </a>
       and written by
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Abel_Santacruz" title="Abel Santacruz">
        Abel Santacruz
       </a>
       . The film stars
       <a href="/wiki/Lolita_Torres" title="Lolita Torres">
        Lolita Torres
       </a>
       ,
       <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Carlos_Estrada_(actor)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Carlos Estrada (actor) (page does not exist)">
        Carlos Estrada
       </a>
       and
       <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Jorge_Barreiro&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Jorge Barreiro (page does not exist)">
        Jorge Barreiro
       </a>
       .

        <a href="#cite_note-1">
         [1]

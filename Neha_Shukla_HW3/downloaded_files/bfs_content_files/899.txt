      <p>
       There are several different types of cost reports.
      </p>
      <p>
       One type of
       <b>
        cost report
       </b>
       is a
       <a href="/wiki/Filmmaking" title="Filmmaking">
        filmmaking
       </a>
       term for a weekly report, compiled by the
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Production_Accountant" title="Production Accountant">
        Production Accountant
       </a>
       , detailing the costs to date, costs this week and estimate of the costs to complete the film.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="/wiki/Medicare_cost_report" title="Medicare cost report">
        Medicare cost reports
       </a>
       must be completed yearly by facilities such as
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Nursing_Homes" title="Nursing Homes">
        nursing homes
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Home_health_nursing" title="Home health nursing">
        home health agencies
       </a>
       , and
       <a href="/wiki/Hospice" title="Hospice">
        hospices
       </a>
       in order to remain compliant with CMS regulations.
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
        <a href="#cite_note-1">
         [1]
        </a>
       </sup>
       They must be filed once a year, generally five months after the close of the facility's
       <a href="/wiki/Fiscal_year" title="Fiscal year">
        fiscal year
       </a>
       .
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">
        <a href="#cite_note-2">
         [2]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>
      <p>
       There are many other types that are related to multiple fields of work.
      </p>

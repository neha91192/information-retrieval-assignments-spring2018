      <p>
       <i>
        <b>
         Closed Door
        </b>
       </i>
       (Spanish:
       <i>
        <b>
         Puerta cerrada
        </b>
       </i>
       ) is a
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/List_of_Argentine_films:1939" title="List of Argentine films:1939">
        1939
       </a>
       <a href="/wiki/Argentina" title="Argentina">
        Argentine
       </a>
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Drama_film" title="Drama film">
        drama film
       </a>
       directed by
       <a href="/wiki/John_Alton" title="John Alton">
        John Alton
       </a>
       and
       <a href="/wiki/Luis_Saslavsky" title="Luis Saslavsky">
        Luis Saslavsky
       </a>
       . The film stars
       <a href="/wiki/Libertad_Lamarque" title="Libertad Lamarque">
        Libertad Lamarque
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Pagano" title="Angelina Pagano">
        Angelina Pagano
       </a>
       and
       <a href="/wiki/Agust%C3%ADn_Irusta" title="Agustín Irusta">
        Agustín Irusta
       </a>
       .
